DEEPER LIFE CHRISTIAN MINISTRY

Deeper life is one of the current bible believing church among all other churches in this time. The church was founded by its general superintendent, Pastor(Dr) Williams Folorunsho Kumuyi in the year 1973 with nothing other than 15 young "truth seekers" at his official university residence,flat 7,which has now been changed to flat 2 as of now. As of today deeper life is among the top three denominational churches to have the highest number of congregation in the world.

Little insight on pastor W.F.Kumuyi

Pastor W.F.Kumuyi was born june 6, in the year 1941 into the family of Mr. Gabriel Kumuyi Akinfenwa and Mrs. Comfort Idowu Kumuyi Akinfenwa. Hailing from Erin-ijesa in oriade local government area of the state of osun, South-western Nigeria, Williams Folorunsho Kumuyi was born into an anglican family of which his parents were consistent worshippers. They made sure that non of their children ever lacked anything spiritually such as early morning devotions, attendance of church activities regularly and so on. Williams folorunsho kumuyi was the first child in a polygamous home of which his mother was also the first wife of his father. Kumuyi has two siblings, Eunice Adewale(Nee Kumuyi) and Samuel Kumuyi Akinfenwa. He is three years older than Eunice and nineteen years older than Samuel. Kumuyi had a zest for music and he was a prolific keyboard player. It was habitual for him to return from school, have his lunch and mount the keyboard. His passion for music and hymn-singing made him to join the choir of The Apostolic Faith Church.

GLOBAL CRUSADES
Deeper life has its weekly service programmes as follows. On sunday the church has its Sunday Worship Service which entails a morning prayer starting from 7:45 - 8:00 before the official commencement of the service. Monday Bible Study is a time of expository searching and teaching of the word of God done by the general superintendent himself. Thursday Revival and Evangelical Training of which the ways of evangelising are taught to the people so as for them to be equipped to expand the kingdom of God. The church has other programmes aside these which are the annual easter and december retreats, monthly weekend revivals, global crusades, success camp programmes for the youths, the children's day programme and so on so forth.

GLOBAL CRUSADE
The first global crusade held by the church was in the year 1985 at the national stadium in lagos themed: "The Great Miracle Crusade". After this there have been series of crusades held by the church in which souls were won and miracles were confirmed. May, in the year 2021, the church started a monthly crusade programme which continued through out the year and into the next and will still continue as the pastor is going abiut both locally and internationally to win more souls into the kingdom. During this crusades, Guest ministers are invited to perform uplifting songs of praises onto the lord. Some of whom are {Don Moen, Nathaniel Bassey, Dunsin Oyekan, e.t.c}
