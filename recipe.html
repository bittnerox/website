RED VELVET CAKE RECIPE

welcome to the all cakes recipe home ,my name is Alagwu Chukwuzurum and as your author i will be giving you the recipe to making a red-velvet cake.

ingredients needed to make the desired cake

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 6 eggs
  • 1/2 carton of milk
  • desired pan shape
  • one cup of baking soda
  • a bunch of strawberries

procedure

  • break eggs into a bowl and mix thoroughly with flour
  • pour milk and mix again
  • slice strawberries and blend into batter
  • pour batter into pan and heat at 350 degrees
  • serve with cold drink

if you follow this steps correctly you should have this as your end result
